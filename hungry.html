I researched, wrote, and scheduled tweets for "Hungry", North by Northwestern’s coverage of the 20th anniversary of a student hunger strike for the Asian American Studies Program at Northwestern University. @NUin1995 tweeted updates in “realtime”, contributing to a package on the strike and its impact.

The Asian American Advisory Board (AAAB) went on hunger strike at @NorthwesternU 20 years ago today. We'll be tweeting it in real-time.

— NU in 1995 (@NUin1995) April 13, 2015

At 11 a.m., 17 students began a hunger strike in order to push @NorthwesternU administration to create an Asian-American studies department.

— NU in 1995 (@NUin1995) April 13, 2015

Inspired by AAAB strike, 17 @Princeton students have staged takeover of offices in protest of lack of curriculum diversity. More to come.

— NU in 1995 (@NUin1995) April 20, 2015

Twelve days after the start of the strike, the last original striker, Charles Chun, has finally dropped out. He has lost 20 pounds.

— NU in 1995 (@NUin1995) April 25, 2015
