I'm a video producer and graphic designer who works in journalism and marketing in New York. I graduated from Northwestern University with a bachelor's in journalism and creative fiction writing.

Right now I'm a producer at Mental Floss, and in the past I've worked at Vox, the Guardian US, the Chicago Reporter, and Penguin Random House. Notable things I've made include this website, a book cover, and breakfast this morning.
